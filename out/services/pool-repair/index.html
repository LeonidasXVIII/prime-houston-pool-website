About pool repair

When something isn't working right — the pump won't prime, pressure is off, the salt system is throwing errors, or you think there's a leak — the goal is a clear diagnosis and a straightforward plan. Pool repairs are available by quote after we take a look at what's actually going on.

What we focus on

  • Equipment troubleshooting — identifying the actual cause, not guessing
  • Pump and filter issues (priming, pressure, leaks at the pad)
  • Salt system and chlorinator performance issues
  • Leaks noticed during service
  • Repair coordination and clear write-ups of what's needed

How pricing works

Repairs are quoted based on the issue, parts needed, and time involved. We'll walk you through the diagnosis and options before any repair work begins.

We don't claim specialty electrical or plumbing licensing unless we've specifically listed it. For work that falls outside our scope, we'll let you know and help coordinate the right licensed trade where needed.
